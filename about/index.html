About

I Computer Science Master’s degree from Stevens Institute of Technology January 2020.

I got my Computer Engineering B.S. degree from UC Davis. The most fun I had there was programming the control logic for my NATCAR project, which was an autonomous small-scale car that raced a track detected by current running through a wire. After my undergrad I entered industry. I worked at an industrial computer and factory automation company for some years. I got a lot of hands-on experience, but found myself wanting to get more into the software side of things and unable to do so at this company. I decided to go back for a Master's degree in Computer Science so that I can get some more software experience besides my home projects.

Besides computers and programming, I really enjoy playing soccer and the occasional game of Dota 2. I'm always up for a challenge in either!
